How to use

  • Join sync: Click the button at the top to start participating.
  • Add Item: Once joined, paste or type multiple lines to add items.
  • Reorder: Drag and drop items in your board to change priority.
  • Copy: Click the clipboard icon on any board to copy its content as Markdown.
  • Clear All: Click the trash icon to reset all items for everyone (use for new discussions).
